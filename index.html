Kiddy TV

YouTube for Kids

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

YouTube videos for your kids.

  • No more worry about inappropriate videos showing up on suggested videos section of YouTube.
  • No need to turn on YouTube safe mode every time you give your device to your kids.

Kiddy TV comes packaged with 100’s of safe, fun, entertaining and educational YouTube videos for your kids. What's more, we arrange these into neat playlists so you don't have to take all that trouble. And on top of that, we keep adding new YouTube videos regularly so your kids can have more new fun everyday. You can also create shortcuts to YouTube videos your kids like by adding them to the Favorite section.

So why not get the app and have complete peace of mind knowing your kids can only watch safe YouTube videos.

Kiddy TV YouTube videos are hand selected by parents like you for all age groups including

  • Infants
  • Toddlers
  • Preschoolers

Key Features of “KiddyTV - YouTube for kids” app:

  • Favorite videos for instant access.
  • Growing collection of 100’s of safe, fun, exciting & educational YouTube videos for your kids.
  • YouTube videos categorized intuitively into playlists.
  • Suggest Youtube videos to us through our FaceBook page
  • Helpful information on making your iPhone kids safe
